Land

SCHEYECHBI used to be the common name for what is now NJ. It is the land of the Lenni-Lenape peoples. The land's original religion believes in a Great Spiriet eleven lessor Gods. Colonialsim destroyed many remnants of the practice but the Land and the Lenape remember. The Treaty of Easton forced the Indigenous of the time out west to what is now Oklahoma. Today there are still Lenapi in NJ here to celebrate, pray, and remember.
